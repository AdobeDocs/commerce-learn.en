# Create Widgets

Widgets are reusable components that you can create and modify to automatically update content across your store. This video shows how to create and manage widgets.

## Who is this video for?

* Website managers
* Content designers

## Video content

* Understand what widgets are and where they appear.
* Create and manage widgets in the Admin.
